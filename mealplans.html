Meal Plans

Ever wished you had someone to plan out your meals according to your dietary needs and preferences? Now you can! Food First Nutrition offers individualized meal plans from Registered Dietitian Avarie Olsen. Meal planning includes a 30-minute consultation to assess dietary needs, preferences, calorie and protein content, and overall cooking lifestyle. Meal plans include 4-weeks of easy 30-minute or less recipes, nutrition information for all the recipes, and week-by-week grocery lists.


Plans start at $100/4-week meal plan. Contact Avarie Olsen for more information.

How Many Servings Do You Need Each Day from the 5 Food Groups?

Food Group # Servings Each Day Equivalent
Grains 5-6 ounces 1 slice bread, 1 cup cereal, 1/2 cup cooked rice
Fruit 1.5- 2 cups 1 cup fruit/100% fruit juice, 1/2 cup dried fruit
Vegetables 2-2.5 cups 1 cup raw or cooked vegetables, 1 cup vegetable juice, 2 cups raw leafy greens
Protein 5-5.5 ounces 1 ounce meat, 1/4 cup cooked beans, 1 egg, 1 tablespoon peanut butter, 1/2 ounce of nuts or seeds
Dairy 3 cups 1 cup milk, yogurt, or soymilk, 1.5 ounces natural cheese, 2 ounces processed cheese
